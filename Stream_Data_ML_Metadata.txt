Metadata for EPA Synoptic Stream Data
May 20, 2020
--------------

siteid - Unique site id for each sample site
lat - Latitude in decimal degrees
long - Longitude in decimal degrees
ecoreg - Omernik Level I Ecoregion
era - Sampling campaign for EPA National Aquatic Resource Surveys
area - Catchment area (km2)
doc - Dissolved organic carbon (mg L-1)
no3 - Nitrate-N (mg L-1)
tn - Total nitrogen (mg L-1)
tp - Total phosphorus (ug L-1)
temp - Mean annual temperature (C)
precip - Mean annual precipitation (mm)
elev - Elevation (m)
forest - Forest cover (%)
wetland - Wetland cover (%)
urban - Urban cover (%)
ag - Agriculture cover (%)
roads - Road density
